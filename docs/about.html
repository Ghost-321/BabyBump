About us

    Who we are

  • We are three Year 11 students who created this website after noticing that many pregnancy websites can be difficult to read and understand. We wanted to create a simple, clear, and reliable resource that makes pregnancy information easier to access. Our website explains the three trimesters of pregnancy in a straightforward way, including important facts, what to expect, and where to get help. We aim to provide information that is easy to follow without overwhelming users with complicated medical language. Through this project, our goal is to help expectant parents, students, and anyone interested in learning about pregnancy find trusted information quickly and confidently.

    • Our Purpose

  • We created this website to provide clear, reliable, and easy-to-understand information about pregnancy. Pregnancy can be an exciting but sometimes overwhelming experience, and it is important to have access to trustworthy information. Our purpose is to help people learn about the changes that occur during pregnancy and to provide guidance that is simple, practical, and easy to follow.

    • What We Provide

  • Our website offers information about all three trimesters of pregnancy. Each section explains what happens during that stage, including physical and emotional changes, baby development, important health tips, and key facts. We also provide information about available support services, healthcare professionals, and resources that can help individuals and families throughout the pregnancy journey.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make pregnancy information accessible to everyone. Whether you are an expectant parent, a student researching pregnancy, or a member of the community looking to learn more, our website is designed to help you find the information you need quickly and easily. We aim to present accurate information in a clear and supportive way so that users can feel informed and confident throughout pregnancy.

Our Commitment

  • We are committed to providing easy-to-read content that helps users better understand pregnancy and where to seek support when needed. By bringing important information together in one place, we hope to encourage healthy pregnancies, informed decision-making, and greater awareness of the resources available to pregnant individuals and their families. Making pregnancy information simple, reliable, accessible, and easy to understand for everyone.
